Who are we

Elegans.io is specialized in physical and mathematical modelling, data analysis, and Monte Carlo simulations.


Wind Power

Elegans.io uses Regional Atmospheric Models with Analog Ensemble, one of the most advanced methods for wind power forecasts.
We produce probabilistic forecasts in order to maximize the income in the day-ahead energy market. In this way traders can value energy price based on the probability of energy production.


Data Science

We share Sherlock Holmes' opinion that it is a capital mistake to theorize before one has data.
We explore data oriented structures to produce high level business models.
